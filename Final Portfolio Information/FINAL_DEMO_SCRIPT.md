# BudgetBrain — Final Demo Script

## Duration: 5-7 minutes

---

### 1. Opening (30 seconds)

"Hello everyone. We're presenting BudgetBrain — a personal finance management web app built for college students. BudgetBrain helps you track expenses, manage budgets, set savings goals, and understand your spending habits through visual analytics."

---

### 2. The Problem (30 seconds)

"Most college students don't have an easy way to track their money. Existing tools are either too complex or require bank integration. BudgetBrain solves this with a simple, all-in-one platform that works in your browser."

---

### 3. Registration and Login (45 seconds)

"Let me show you how it works. First, you register with your name, email, and password. [Navigate to /register] After registration, you're redirected to login. Once logged in, you land on the dashboard."

---

### 4. Dashboard Overview (45 seconds)

"This is the dashboard. It shows your total budgets, total expenses, active savings goals, and remaining balance. You can see your recent transactions and a budget breakdown. Currency exchange rates are shown here — you can change your preferred currency in settings."

---

### 5. Budgets (45 seconds)

"Let's look at budgets. [Navigate to Budgets page] You can create budgets by category — like Food, Rent, or Entertainment. Each budget has a name, amount, and color. The progress bar shows how much you've spent versus your limit. You can edit or delete budgets anytime."

---

### 6. Expenses (45 seconds)

"Under each budget, you can add expenses. [Add a sample expense] Enter the name, amount, and it's recorded. The transaction list shows all your expenses sorted by date. You can edit or delete individual expenses."

---

### 7. Savings Goals (45 seconds)

"BudgetBrain also lets you set savings goals. [Navigate to Goals page] Create a goal like 'New Laptop' with a target amount and optional deadline. As you save money, you add to your progress. The progress bar shows you how close you are to your goal."

---

### 8. Analytics (45 seconds)

"The analytics page gives you visual insights. [Navigate to Analytics page] The pie chart shows your spending by category. The bar chart shows spending trends over time. This helps you understand where your money is going."

---

### 9. Profile and Theme (30 seconds)

"In your profile, you can change your avatar and display name. [Navigate to Profile] In Settings, you can toggle between dark and light themes. The theme persists even after you reload the page."

---

### 10. Receipt Scanning (30 seconds)

"BudgetBrain also supports receipt scanning. You can upload a receipt image, and the app extracts text using OCR — either through our PaddleOCR service or browser-based Tesseract fallback."

---

### 11. Deployment (15 seconds)

"The app is live at budgetbrain.vercel.app. The frontend is built with React and Vite, the backend runs on Express, data is stored in MongoDB Atlas, and everything is deployed through Vercel."

---

### 12. Closing (15 seconds)

"That's BudgetBrain — a complete, production-ready budget management app. Thank you for watching. We're happy to answer any questions."
